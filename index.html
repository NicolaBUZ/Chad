The Origin Story

From Zero to Billion-Dollar Bathroom

Chad Rollington was just 19 years old when he had the revelation that would change the world — and every bathroom in it. Sitting in a dormitory restroom, he noticed the toilet paper dispenser had no idea how much paper he needed. "Inefficiency," he later wrote in his bestselling memoir, The Unrolling.

Two years later, FlushAI™ was born. What started as a $47 prototype built from a Raspberry Pi and a shattered iPhone became the most disruptive bathroom-technology company in human history.

Chad has since been featured in Forbes, TechCrunch, and The Bathroom Technology Quarterly. He drives three Teslas simultaneously.

"People said disrupting the toilet paper industry was impossible. That's because they were thinking inside the stall."

— Chad Rollington, TED Talk: "Wipe Smarter, Not Harder" (2023)
